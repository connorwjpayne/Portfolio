About Me

I graduated from Western University with a major in Physical Geography and the Environment, where I developed a strong foundation in spatial thinking, environmental analysis, and geographic problem-solving. I am currently enrolled in a postgraduate Geographic Information Systems (GIS) certification program, where I am expanding my technical skills in geospatial analysis, data management, and web-based mapping applications.

My academic and professional interests include environmental analysis, urban planning, spatial data acquisition, remote sensing, and effective management of geospatial data. I am particularly interested in how GIS and WebGIS technologies can be used to support informed decision-making related to environmental sustainability and urban development.

Outside of academics, I am an outdoor-oriented individual who enjoys exploring natural environments and understanding landscapes through both field experience and spatial data. This combination of technical training and personal interest in the environment motivates my pursuit of a career in GIS and WebGIS development, where I aim to create meaningful, data-driven solutions to real-world challenges.
